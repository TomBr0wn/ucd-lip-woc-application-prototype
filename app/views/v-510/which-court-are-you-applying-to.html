Apply to the county court for a warrant of control

You can apply to the county court to collect your money. This is called a 'warrant of control' application.

The court fee is £83 to apply. The court will try to recover the money you're owed, including the fee, by giving {{ data['defendant-name'] }} a deadline for payment.

If they do not pay in time, a county court bailiff will visit them to try to collect either:

  • payment for the money you're owed
  • items of the same value to sell at auction
