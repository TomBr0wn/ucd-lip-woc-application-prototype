What happens next

We’ll check your application and may contact you if we have any questions.

We’ll email you once your application is accepted. The court will then contact {{ data['defendant-name'] }} to try to recover the money by either:

  • asking them to pay in full
  • agreeing a 3-month payment plan

The court will send you the money once they receive it. For example, at the end of the 3-month payment plan.

If {{ data['defendant-name'] }} does not pay, a bailiff will visit them to ask for payment or collect any items to sell at auction for the same value. A warrant of control is valid for one year. A bailiff may make up to 3 visits over this time.
