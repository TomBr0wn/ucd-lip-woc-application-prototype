Ask the court to try to recover your money

You can ask the court to try and get your money back if [defendant name] has not paid you.

You cannot apply if they’ve entered breathing space.

You’ll need to pay a fee to apply – the court adds this fee to the money owed to you. The court cannot guarantee you’ll get your money back.

Find out:

  • your options for recovering your money
  • how to apply – online or by post

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
