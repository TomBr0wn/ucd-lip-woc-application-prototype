Apply to recover your money

You can apply to the court to ‘enforce your judgment’. This is when the court tries to recover the money you’re owed from {{ data['defendant-name'] }}.

The court cannot guarantee you’ll get the money back.

Use this service to:

  • find out your options for enforcing your judgment
  • apply to the court – depending on the option you choose, you might need to apply by post

You cannot apply to enforce your judgment if you’re told that {{ data['defendant-name'] }} has got a ‘breathing space’ and is temporarily protected. Find out about your responsibilities during a breathing space (opens in a new window).

You’ll need to pay a court fee to apply. The court will then try to recover this fee, and the money you’re owed, from {{ data['defendant-name'] }}.
