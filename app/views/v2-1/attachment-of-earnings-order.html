Apply to deduct money from {{ data['defendant-name'] }}’s wages

You can ask the court to deduct money from {{ data['defendant-name'] }}’s wages, through their employer. This is called an 'attachment of earnings order'.

{{ data['defendant-name'] }} must be in paid employment to apply. They cannot be unemployed or self-employed.

The application costs £119. The court adds this fee to the total amount owed to you.
