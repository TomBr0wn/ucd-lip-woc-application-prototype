How do you want to apply to the court to collect your money?

The court will try to recover your money by giving [defendant’s name] a deadline for paying you. If they do not pay you in time, a bailiff will visit them to collect the money or items.

The bailiff will be:

  • a county court bailiff if you apply for a warrant of control
  • a high court bailiff if you apply for a writ of control
