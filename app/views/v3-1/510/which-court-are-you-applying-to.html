Which court are you applying to?

The court will try to recover your money by giving {{ data['defendant-name'] }} a deadline for payment. If they do not pay in time, a high court or county court bailiff will visit to try to collect the money or items to sell at auction.

The court you need to apply to depends on the total amount of money you’re owed. This includes the court fee of either:

  • £71 if you apply to the high court
  • £83 if you apply to the county court
